## Background

Founded by Adam Morse, Array Engineering provides advanced PV and Battery design and microgrid engineering services — combined with software development capability and visualization.

My focus is to make distributed energy resources understandable and more controllable through rigorous technical analysis and purpose-built software.

The energy transition demands that we crack the data science, modeling and control challenge in short order.

## Expertise

- Solar PV system design and engineering
- Battery energy storage systems (BESS)
- Microgrid feasibility and optimization
- DER software and analytics (Python, HoloViz)
- Power systems and interconnection standards
